Research:

My research focuses on understanding contemporary design approaches and how visual identity can communicate emotion and purpose. I draw inspiration from designers such as Jessica Walsh, Tobias van Schneider, Poster Boy, and Studio Up North, whose distinctive styles influence my experimentation with colour, layout, and bold conceptual ideas. Studying their methods helps me refine my own creative direction and develop designs that feel expressive, modern, and purposeful. Through ongoing research and exploration, I aim to strengthen my skills and create work that reflects both originality and professional insight and growth.

Reflection:

Creating my website was a challenging but rewarding experience that helped me develop both technical and creative skills. At the beginning of the project, I had only a basic idea of what I wanted to build, but through planning and experimentation I was able to shape a clearer vision. I learned how important layout, navigation, and visual design are in making a site user-friendly. I also improved my coding skills and grew more confident using HTML, CSS, and other tools. Although I faced difficulties along the way, overcoming them strengthened my understanding and made the final outcome more meaningful.
